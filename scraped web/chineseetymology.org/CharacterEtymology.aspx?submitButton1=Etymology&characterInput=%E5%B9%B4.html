ShuoWen: (說文解字):
穀孰也從禾千聲春秋傳曰大有年

New! etymology explanation under construction.

Primitive pictograph 年. A harvist of grain 禾 and phonetic (also man carrying the grain) 人 . Original meaning year end harvist. Meaning year.
