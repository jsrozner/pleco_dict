ShuoWen: (說文解字):
木星也越歷二十八宿宣徧陰陽十二月一次從步戌聲律歷書名五星為五步

New! etymology explanation under construction.

Super cursive remnant 岁(山夕)歲. From remnant 山 and remnant 夕. From walking 步 (through the fields) and phonetic (also meaning a scythe used to cut the harvist) 戌. Original meaning harvist.
